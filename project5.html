Delicious Tacos

A simple and flavorful taco recipe perfect for a quick meal.

Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time:

10 mins

Servings:

4

Ingredients:

  • 8 small tortillas
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup cheese, grated
  • 1 tbsp taco seasoning
  • Salsa and sour cream to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion and taco seasoning, and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Warm the tortillas in a separate pan.
  4. Assemble the tacos by adding beef, lettuce, tomato, and cheese to each tortilla.
  5. Top with salsa and sour cream as desired. Serve and enjoy!
